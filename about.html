Mission & Vision

Our mission is to uplift local farmers by connecting them directly with customers through a transparent, tech-enabled marketplace. We envision a Nepal where agriculture thrives through digital innovation, fair pricing, and community trust.

Digital Agriculture in Nepal

Digital agriculture empowers farmers with better market access, reduced middlemen, and data-driven insights for sustainable growth. It ensures fresher produce for customers and stronger rural economies.

Benefits for Farmers & Customers

Farmers gain tools for sales tracking, pricing updates, and order management. Customers enjoy seasonal, locally sourced foods with transparent billing and farmer ratings for confidence.

Together, we cultivate a smarter food system.
